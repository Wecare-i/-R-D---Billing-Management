Hello Hieu Le Hoang,

To assist with migrating your legacy MOSA billing account to an MCA billing account and consolidating your subscriptions, please note the following steps based on Microsoft procedures:

**1. Confirm the types of your billing accounts** and ensure you have the required administrative roles. For MOSA billing accounts, you must be at least a Billing Administrator, and for MCA billing accounts, you need to have roles such as Billing Account Owner or Contributor to make billing or payment changes.

**2. To update the payment method on your MOSA account** and stop charges to a personal card, add your corporate payment method in the Microsoft 365 admin center under Billing > Bills & payments > Payment methods. After adding the corporate card, replace the existing payment method on the MOSA account with the new corporate card by using the Replace payment method option and following the wizard to confirm the subscriptions to move to the corporate card.

**3. For consolidating subscriptions under an MCA billing account**, you will need to purchase equivalent subscriptions under the target MCA billing account, verify that both old and new subscriptions are active and that you have enough licenses, then move users from the MOSA subscription to the new MCA subscription. Afterward, cancel or remove licenses from the old subscription as needed.

These procedures will help you migrate your MOSA subscriptions to MCA, update payment methods, consolidate invoices, and update your billing account name accordingly without service disruption.

Hi Hieu Hoang,

Thank you for your detailed update and questions.

**1. Update Payment Method (MOSA):**
For updating the payment method on your MOSA account, you can indeed proceed directly from the Microsoft 365 Admin Center. Navigate to **Billing > Bills & payments > Payment methods**. Here, you can add your corporate MasterCard as a new payment method. After adding it, you can replace the existing personal Visa card with the new corporate card by selecting the **Replace payment method** option and following the wizard to confirm the subscriptions to move to the new payment method. This process applies to MOSA billing accounts and allows you to replace the payment method associated with one or more subscriptions without deleting the old payment method, which remains available for other uses if needed.

**2. Consolidate Subscriptions (MOSA → MCA):**
Regarding consolidating subscriptions from MOSA to MCA billing accounts, **there is no direct transfer method available**. The recommended approach is to purchase equivalent subscriptions under your MCA billing account, ensure both old and new subscriptions are active with sufficient licenses, then move users from the MOSA subscriptions to the new MCA subscriptions. Afterward, you can cancel or remove licenses from the old MOSA subscriptions as needed.

**3. Bill-To Name Update:**
For updating the Bill-To company name on your MOSA account, you can change the billing address details in the Microsoft 365 Admin Center. For MOSA billing accounts, go to the admin center, then **Billing > Your products**, select the subscription, and in the **Service usage address** section, select **Edit service usage address**. Update the organization information to reflect the new company name "Công ty cổ phần Wecare Group" and save the changes. This will help your accounting department process invoices under the correct company name.

Please let me know if you need further assistance with these steps.
